Youpi !

1991

  • First release
  • Originally developed for the Amiga, Atari ST and PC

What is the goal ?

To complete the level, the player must guide a certain percentage of the lemmings from the entrance to the exit with specific skills that can be assigned to them (“Climbers”, “Bombers”, “Diggers”...). Of course, the number of uses of each skill varies from level to level !
